Steps on how to make drip coffee:

  1. Measure and grind some coffee beans.
  2. Place the filter on the brewer.
  3. Add the coffee grounds on the filter.
  4. Pour the hot water slowly over the grounds.
  5. Play guitar while the coffee drips.
  6. Agitate the brew with a gentle stir.
  7. Avoid using the last few drops.
